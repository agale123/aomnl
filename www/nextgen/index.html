Next Generation

These quilts are made by Carolyn's granddaughter, Alison Gale. Carolyn taught her how to quilt as a child and gifted her one of her old sewing machines to continue quilting.

Bear Applique (2019)

This piece was made using a kit from Northern Threads. It was my first experience backing and binding a quilt.

Kauai Quilts (2020)

I picked up some beautiful batiks from a store called Kapaia Stitchery in Kauai. I experimented with several designs like making hearts and pinwheels. Machine quilted by Northern Threads.

National Parks (2021)

A panel quilt featuring pictuers from all the National Parks. This is backed in a plaid flannel. My first experience tying a quilt.

Moose Applique (2021)

This piece was my second one made using a kit from Northern Threads.

Double Diamond (2021)

This was the first quilt top I finished back in 2019 utilizing batik strip packs. I saved the quilt top so I could use it to learn long arm quilting. I was originally going to learn in 2020, but it got delayed until 2021 due to Coronavirus.

Penguin Party (2021)

I found this super cute penguin pattern from Elizabeth Hartman. I loved picking out the fabrics for the sweaters. This was the second quilt that I quilted myself on a long arm machine.

Nine Patch (2021)

Wanted to try a non-square nine patch pattern. Mostly used fabrics from Hawaii and mixed them with some black and white fabrics from my grandmother.
